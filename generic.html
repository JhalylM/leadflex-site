How LeadFlex Works

Your automated AI assistant for gym lead generation.

Step 1: We Train Your AI Assistant

We gather your gym’s information—pricing, hours, location, class schedules, amenities, and FAQs. The AI is fully trained to answer all prospect questions accurately and professionally.

Step 2: Deploy on Your Website

Your chatbot goes live with copy-and-paste installation, available on all devices.

Step 3: Leads Are Captured Automatically

Prospects are guided to provide their name, phone, and email. Every lead is stored in your dashboard instantly.

Step 4: Automated Follow-Ups

The AI sends reminders for tours, trial passes, and information requests. No human follow-up is needed.

Step 5: You Convert More Members

Your team focuses on tours and sign-ups while LeadFlex handles everything else.
